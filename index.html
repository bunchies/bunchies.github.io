IRC (Live Chat)

#bunchies @ Rizon
ip6 is also available at #bunchies @ Rizon (IP6)
More information about the server, including a web interface to connect is available at Rizon.net

I highly recommend registering your username when you connect; this will enable you to register your own channel if you want and get channel benefits (and the channel may be set to registered users only if necessary). There's a bunch of cool stuff you can do with NickServ too, such as a custom vhost.

Be sure to check out the Rizon wiki for help with various commands relating to IRC.
